Take Me Back To The Night We Met

Ditulis oleh Nada Cantika, pada 27 Januari 2023

In the twilight's embrace, their love did bloom,
two souls entwined, in passion's sweet perfume.
Her heart whispered secrets, his echoed the same,
bound by love's thread, they danced in flame.

Through starlit nights and sun-kissed days,
their love blossomed in countless ways.
Hand in hand, they faced the world's test,
in each other's arms, they found their rest.

But seasons change, and so did they,
their love, once fierce, began to sway.
Words left unspoken, tears fell like rain,
as their once vibrant love began to wane.

The day arrived when they said goodbye,
each tear shed, a silent cry.
She longed to return to that fateful night,
where their love sparkled in the moon's soft light.

Yet, time marches on, memories fade,
and what once was, begins to degrade.
She's left with echoes of a love that's gone,
in the stillness of night, she's all alone.

So she holds onto moments, fleeting and rare,
a bittersweet reminder of love's tender care.
Though the path ahead may seem dark and steep,
in her heart, the memories of love will forever keep.

I am not the only traveler,
who has not repaid his debt.
I've been searching for a trail to follow again.
Take me back to the night we met.

Then I can tell myself,
what the hell I'm supposed to do.
Then I can tell myself,
not to ride along with you.

I had all and then most of you
some and now none of you.
Take me back to the night we met.

I don't know what I'm supposed to do,
haunted by the ghost of you.
Oh, take me back to the night we met.

When the night was full of terror,
and your eyes were filled with tears,
when you had not touched me yet.
Oh! Take me back to the night we met.

I had all and then most of you
some and now none of you.
Take me back to the night we met.
I don't know what I'm supposed to do
haunted by the ghost of you,
take me back to the night we met.
